About Lavian Dsouza

Hello! I’m Lavian Dsouza, the creator of BeyondEquation. This blog is my space to share thoughts, images, and simple HTML games I’ve crafted. I’m passionate about exploring creativity through writing and coding, often blending the two in fun ways.

BeyondEquation started as a personal project to document my adventures—whether it’s imagining cosmic journeys, tinkering with pixel art, or baking (sometimes disastrous) cakes. I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoy creating it!

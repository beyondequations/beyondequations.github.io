Collatz Conjecture Analysis

Published: March 01, 2025 | Category: Mathematics

Diving into the unsolved mystery of the Collatz Conjecture, where any positive integer eventually reaches 1 through the rules: if even, divide by 2; if odd, multiply by 3 and add 1.

\( n \to \begin{cases} n/2 & \text{if } n \text{ is even} \\ 3n + 1 & \text{if } n \text{ is odd} \end{cases} \)

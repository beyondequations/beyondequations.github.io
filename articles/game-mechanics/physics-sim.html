Simulating Game Physics

Published: March 05, 2025 | Category: Game Mechanics & Analysis

Using basic physics, I built a 2D ball bounce simulation. The velocity update is \( v = v_0 - g \cdot t \), where \( g = 9.8 \, \text{m/s}^2 \). It’s a simple yet fun way to test collision detection.
